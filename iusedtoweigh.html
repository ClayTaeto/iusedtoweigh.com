My Story

Hi my name is Luke Whitten. I started this company because, like many people, I used to weigh a lot more than I do now. In fact, I used to weigh 280 lbs. I now weigh around 200 lbs, and I'm still losing weight. I was inspired to print this message on a shirt I could wear and show people where I started. It would be like being your own real life after picture. When you put a lot of hard work in, you can feel good about showing it off. So, grab a shirt, and tell people what you used to weigh.
